### What I do

I work across the full lifecycle of enterprise software — from keeping JDK 1.8 monoliths alive with JSF and PL/SQL, to designing new services with Spring Boot, Angular, Docker, and RabbitMQ. I'm most valuable where old systems meet new requirements.

Outside of work, I solder ESP32 boards, train CNNs, and automate things people still do by hand.
